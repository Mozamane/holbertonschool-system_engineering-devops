About Us (:

"Medicines cure diseases, but only doctors can cure patients."



We are a small IT startup company launched this year 2022 with a strong belief in helping improve the level of service to patients whilst helping doctors with the day to day difficulties of running their practices. Both doctors and patients face a similar problem but from different sides of the fence. Patients are looking for good doctors with modern services whilst doctors are becoming ever more competitive and looking for more efficient ways to fill their appointment slots. Hence DOBS.



Doctor Online booking system is a free online booking service for medical practitioners. You simply search for a practitioner, make a bookings. Its free, convenient and available 24-7. Unlike many medical directories online, medapp offers a real-time calendar bookings.
